🚫 Meta Questions Suck

Don't ask “Can I ask something?” or “Is it okay to ask about X?”

Just ask the goddamn question. If it’s dumb, we’ll tell you. But wasting time with a meta-question is worse.

💥 Why Meta Questions Are Bad

1. They waste time: Every second spent on "Can I ask something?" is a second lost. People tune out.

2. They add no value: Meta questions don't contain useful info. They're just noise in the signal.

3. They block the flow: Conversations stall while everyone waits for you to say the actual thing.

4. They confuse noobs: Beginners see meta questions and think it's the norm. It lowers the quality of future questions.

5. They invite spam: Instead of useful answers, you get “yes?”, “go ahead”, or nothing at all.

6. They’re insecure as hell: If you're asking permission to speak, you're not confident. Just speak up and let the answer sort itself out.
