YourName - Stop Asking Meta Questions

I make games, sometimes break them, and hate meta questions with a passion.

🚫 Meta Questions Suck

Don't ask “Can I ask something?” or “Is it okay to ask about X?”

Just ask the goddamn question. If it’s dumb, we’ll tell you. But wasting time with a meta-question is worse.

💥 Why Meta Questions Are Bad

1. They waste time:
Every second spent on “Can I ask something?” is a second lost. People tune out.
2. They add no value:
Meta questions don’t give useful information. They’re just noise.
3. They block the flow:
Conversations stall while people wait for the real question. It’s annoying.
4. They confuse noobs:
Newbies copy what they see. If they copy meta-question nonsense, the whole community drops in quality.
5. They invite spam:
You get pointless replies like “yes?”, “go ahead”, or silence.
6. They’re insecure as hell:
If you’re asking for permission to speak, you’re broadcasting fear. Just say it.
